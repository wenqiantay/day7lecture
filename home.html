HaciPupu is a series of collectible toys from the Chinese toy company Pop Mart: Description The Hacipupu toys are cute, hand-painted figures that are between 2.76 and 3.54 inches (7cm to 9cm) tall. The toys are made from PVC, ABS, and paper materials, and are odorless and harmless. Packaging The toys come in sealed boxes called "blind boxes", which means the buyer doesn't know what's inside until they open it. Some sets may include a "secret" or "chase" figure, which is rarer to find than the "basic" figures. Uses The toys can be used as home decor, party favors, car accessories, or office items. They can also be shared with family and friends. Series There are several Hacipupu series, including The Kindergarten Day Series, My Little Hero, The Constellation Series, In My Dream, and more. Where to buy You can buy Hacipupu toys from Pop Mart stores, online stores, and other retailers.
